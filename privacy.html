Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

NUTS Tips ("we", "our", "us") operates automated social media accounts that publish football prediction content. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our use of third-party platform APIs (including TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube).

1. Information We Collect

Our application does not collect personal information from end users. We only use platform APIs to publish content (videos and captions) that we create ourselves to our own connected social media accounts.

2. How We Use Platform APIs

We use official APIs (such as the TikTok Content Posting API, Instagram Graph API, and YouTube Data API) solely to automate the publishing of our own pre-produced video content to our own accounts. No third-party user data is accessed, stored, or shared.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens required to authenticate with these platforms are stored securely within our automation infrastructure and are not shared with any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

Our automation may use third-party services (such as cloud rendering or AI content generation tools) strictly to produce our own video content before publishing. These services do not receive any personal data belonging to platform users.
